# 🚌 College Bus Tracker

**Know where your bus is. Always.**

Welcome to the College Bus Tracker – your go-to app for real-time bus locations, detailed schedules, and instant contact with the transport department. Built for students, parents, and staff of Dr. K. Vasudevan College of Engineering and Technology (Prince Group).

---

## 📱 What You Can Do

- 🗺️ **Live Bus Tracking** – Watch your bus move on the map in real time.
- 🚏 **Browse All Routes** – See every route, first pickup, and driver details.
- ⏰ **Full Schedules** – Check exact pickup times for every stop on your route.
- 📞 **Get Help Instantly** – Helpline, email, and office address right at your fingertips.
- 📢 **Stay Updated** – Service announcements and delays appear on the home screen.

## 🏠 Home Page

When you open the app, you'll land here:

- **Start Tracking** – One click takes you straight to the live map.
- **Quick Actions** – Jump to Live Map, Routes, or Schedules with one tap.
- **Announcements** – Important updates from the transport office.
- **Contact Info** – Helpline number and office hours always visible.

---

## 🗺️ Live Tracking

See every active bus on a map.

1. Choose a route from the dropdown (or see all at once).
2. Each bus appears as a colored marker – click it to see route name and speed.
3. The map updates every 10 seconds automatically.
4. Use **Refresh** to update positions manually.

> 💡 The legend at the bottom helps you match colors to route numbers.

---

## 🚏 Route Directory

Find your bus route quickly.

- All 12+ routes are shown as cards.
- Each card tells you:
  - Route number and area
  - First stop and pickup time
  - Driver name and contact
  - In‑charge staff member

Click any card – it highlights for easy reference.

---

## ⏱️ Detailed Schedules

Know exactly when the bus reaches your stop.

- **Search** by route number or area name (e.g., "4", "Red Hills").
- Tap **"Show stops ▼"** on any route card to expand the full timetable.
- Every stop and its scheduled time is listed clearly.
- Driver and in‑charge phone numbers are included – call if you need live updates.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: The bus icon isn't moving. Is it broken?**  
A: Not at all. The map refreshes every 10 seconds. If the bus is at a stop or in traffic, it will appear stationary. Hit **Refresh** to force an update.

**Q: How do I know which bus is mine?**  
A: Check your assigned route number (available from the college office). On the tracking page, use the legend to find your route's color.

**Q: The schedule times seem off.**  
A: The timetables shown are for **morning pick‑up** effective from 02.01.2026. For afternoon or special schedules, please contact the transport office.

**Q: I found a mistake. Whom should I tell?**  
A: Please use the contact form or call the helpline. We appreciate your help in keeping information accurate.
